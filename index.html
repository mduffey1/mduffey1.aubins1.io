McDougall Insurance is a locally owned, independent insurance agency. We succeed because we provide the right protection for our clients while delivering seamless customer service. Our agency is recognized for its integrity, honesty and commitment in everything we do. Our core strategy is to build long lasting relationships with our customers by acting in their best interest.
Our overall mission is simple, provide our customers with a consultative approach to determine the most comprehensive products to protect your assets. Having the peace of mind to know that you, your family, and your assets are protected is what’s most important to us.
McDougall Insurance is a totally independent insurance agency, free to choose the best carriers for our client’s needs. We do not work for the insurance company, we work for you, our customers.
We represent a select group of financially strong insurance companies, therefore, we are able to offer our customers the best coverage at the most competitive price. Having a staff with over 20 years experience gives us the ability to further enhance the customer experience and relationship.
We look forward to doing business with you.
